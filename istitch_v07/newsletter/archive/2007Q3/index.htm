going commercial

It's been a good run for Solana Beach-based photographer Kevin Roche. With more than 10 years of experience as a photographer in the field, Roche has operated a successful photography business, traveled the world and captured images of a diverse range of subject matter. His fresh style and technical skill have allowed him to branch into such areas as the action sports industry, retail and corporate photography. Recently, he decided to pair up with istitch to revitalize his commercial photography brand.

The project involved the design of original branding elements that were incorporated into a new website for the business. "One of the things we wanted to achieve was a design that would highlight the color and texture of the images in a sophisticated way." says Roche. "We also wanted something that would emphasize the commercial aspect of the business--thereís a difference between this type of photography and fine art."

Neutral elements highlight the color and composition of each image.

With an existing logo already being used in other aspects of Rocheís photography business, the tailored solution was the addition of a "bar code" graphic that was incorporated on the site and supporting collateral. The graphic allows each piece to stand out as a "commercial" work while also providing the businessís phone number. The new element, sophisticated look and simple navigation, allow images to stand out, with vivid color and composition.

The newly revamped site provides Roche's commercial photography business a promotional framework with which to develop and maintain new ventures. Separating the work into conceptual categories allows art directors and others to gain a sense of how Roche's unique style might be incorporated into new concepts for particular projects they might have in mind. "There's an emphasis on the potential to communicate a particular message." Roche said about the presentation of his work.

To contact Kevin Roche, call 858 755 6060, or view the website by clicking here. He is available for commercial, editorial and stock assignments worldwide. Roche holds a bachelor's degree in visual arts from the University of California, San Diego and is a member of the American Society of Media Photographers.

summer reading

With summertime in full affect, you may hopefully still have a chance to catch up on some reading. If so, here's a recent release that is sure to help you maintain a fresh outlook on life.

American Home Life, a fictional novel by David Barringer, captures the essence of contemporary life in postmodern society with its snappy prose and schizophrenic wit. The ever-shifting attention span of the novel's central character and narrator soars across the panorama of pop-culture, geo-politics, psychology and natural science, all while maintaining a playful focus on the challenge of raising two children. Beneath the whimsy, serious insight is revealed about the fears and turmoil associated with a contemporary couple trying to prevent their children from being trapped by greed, envy and the ever-present monotony of suburban life.

Published by So New Media, American Home Life is available for purchase at websites for So New, Powells, or Amazon. To see more of Barringerís work, log on to www.davidbarringer.com.
